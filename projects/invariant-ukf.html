Invariant UKF for Distributed Robotic Systems

Multi-agent target tracking on matrix Lie groups with inverse covariance intersection sensor fusion.

Topics: Matrix Lie groups, multi-agent SLAM, sensor fusion

Highlights

  • Proposed a target tracking algorithm for distributed time-variant systems in 3D with an invariant UKF (using matrix Lie groups and Lie algebra) for distributed state estimation
  • Applied the Inverse Covariance Intersection sensor fusion algorithm as a consistent and less conservative method for handling unknown distributed covariance
  • Performed 50 trials of Monte-Carlo simulation at different communication rates (including a centralized system) and computed position and orientation RMSE to show the algorithm is robust to time-varying communication topology
